What is this?


It's a tool to more easily and efficiently plan your class schedule. We've created a fluid and fast interface to the class catalog, with flexible filtering, sorting, and intuitive planning. Once you've planned your semester, we'll save it for you.

On your registration date, return here and login, and you'll be able to add your entire courseload with the click of a single button. We hope this helps you get into all your classes without scrambling through the catalog last-minute.
